    HackJos

    HackJos is a challenge-driven hackathon and conference that was created to bring together tech-savvy citizens, scientists, startups, entrepreneurs, educators, students, programmers and software developers across Nigeria under one roof for 48 hours to get involved and showcase their ingenuity and talents in the pursuit of creating compelling solutions that cut across mobile, desktop, web and hardware (embedded systems) that will help solve problems relevant to the various sectors of the Nigerian economy.
